What is Cantaloupe?

It is a small round melon of a variety with orange flesh and ribbed skin.

Reasons to Love Cantaloupe

Fun Facts about Cantaloupe
  • You can add it to smoothies, salsa, salad, etc.

  • It is high in fiber and moisture so it prevents constipation.

  • It is high in water and prevents dehydration.

  • Cantaloupe is packed with Vitamin A and antioxidants
    such as beta-carotene, lutein, zea-xanthin and cryptoxanthin.
    That means protection against colon,prostate, breast,
    endometrial, lung, and pancreatic cancers.
  • It has a lot of Vitamin C.

  • Cantaloupe is an annual plant.

  • Cantaloupe is also known as muskmelon in the USA.

  • Cantaloupe was very popular and often cultivated in the ancient Egypt.

  • Cantaloupe are members of a vine-crop family known as Cucurbitaceae,
    which includes other melons, squash, cucumbers, pumpkins, and gourds.
