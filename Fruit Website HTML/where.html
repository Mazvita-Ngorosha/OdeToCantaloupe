Where to buy Cantaloupe

According to the USDA Economic Research Service (ERS),
the average retail price of a cantaloupe is $0.54 per pound.

In the U.S., cantaloupe's best season is from June till August.
The rest of the year, cantaloupe is generally shipped from
South America, so by the time it's in stores, it will have been
off the vine for a while.

You can purchase cantaloupe at these grocery stores:

Kroger

ALDI

Whole Foods

Walmart

Meijer

You can also purchase cantaloupe at your local Farmer's Market.
